# Border War Defender Fix

Standalone CK3 1.19.* mod that makes border-war protection apply per sub-realm instead of treating every vassal under a huge top liege as equally exposed.

The mod lets title wars remain declarable against a lower vassal, but warns in the declaration screen when a named higher intermediate liege is still small enough to protect that title. The warning appears as an icon on affected war-goal title rows; hover it to see the exact protector who will be added. On war start, an on-action fallback adds the highest eligible intermediate protector as a defender.
